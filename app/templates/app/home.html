We are a Knowledge based society in Royal Kavery Thanjavur

Business Club, Royal Kavery Thanjavur, also known as RVClub, is a student-run organization that provides a platform for students to learn about and explore the world of business. It is one of the largest and most active student organizations on campus, with over 1000 members.

The business club bridges the gap between business and technology, equipping students with skills and knowledge to succeed in the business world. It organizes various events, including workshops, seminars, webinars, knowledge meets, and case competitions.

Business Club has three wings: Strategy, Finance, and Analytics. Each wing focuses on a different aspect of business and offers students opportunities to learn from industry experts and gain hands-on experience.
